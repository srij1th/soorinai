How to Play Chess

1. Starting a Game

  • Go to the Dashboard and click Play as White or Play as Black.
  • If you choose White, you will move first. If you choose Black, wait for the AI to make its opening move.

2. How to Move

  • To move, click on a piece you want to move.
  • Highlighted squares will show all valid moves.
  • Click a highlighted square to move the piece there.

3. Timer Rules

  • Each game has a 4-minute timer (240 seconds).
  • The timer starts after the first move is made (by either player or AI).
  • If the time runs out, the game ends immediately.

4. Points System

  • Points are awarded for capturing opponent pieces:
  • Pawn = 1, Knight = 3, Bishop = 3, Rook = 5, Queen = 9

5. Win Conditions

A player can win in one of two ways:

  • Checkmate: Trap the opponent's king so it cannot escape.
  • Timeout: When the 4-minute timer ends, the player with the higher points wins. If points are equal, it's a Draw.
